About

 Edward Rudolph is a professional bassist, composer, and scholar. He has been playing music for over sixteen years and has performed at various events and festivals internationally and around the midwest in groups ranging is size from big bands to trios. Ed specializes in Jazz, but enjoys playing a spectrum of genres such as classical, hip-hop, bluegrass, funk, folk, and world music.

 At 25 years old, Rudolph has had the privilege of working with many legendary and award winning musicians such as Bill Pierce, Darren Barrett, Kevin Harris, Jason Squinobal, Dennis Mackrel, Gary Smulyan and Steve Davis.
